    • Notes from the Day

    Natasha recommended "Crowdsourced research: Many hands make tight work" by Raphael Silberzahn and Eric L. Uhlmann describing research that is conducted and reviewed by several teams, all sharing ideas, methods, and so on, to achieve better results.

    Mark Fernandes mentioned Docker on Windows via the Docker Toolbox (formerly Kitematic).

    He also passed along via email a joint MRC/BBSRC report on Reproducible Research in Biomedical sciences that came out this October.
